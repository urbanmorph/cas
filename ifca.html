Innovation for Clean Air

Testbed

The clean air street is being piloted by Directorate of Urban Land Transport in association with Catapult UK and Urban Morph to enable innovators who are working on green technologies to showcase their products and test their efficacy. This living lab is a part of the Testbed strategy being adopted by DULT to encourage innovation in the adoption of walking, cycling, public transport, electric non-motorised vehicles & shared mobility.
